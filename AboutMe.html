Editing is such a big part of the things I like, even if like all things I enjoy I can't seem to find time for it any more. I'd like to say my knack for it came from my Grandma, she's always been big on Photography and Computers herself so it's not very difficult to trace the path down to me. Gameplay Highlights/Funny Moments are my bread and butter with Content since it's just what i find comes to me the most naturally humour wise, when you're editing a video in that style you get to be really creative with what edits you actually make and that's amplified 10 fold if the person you're editing for interacts with you through the recording.

Maybe I'd have a better time with Plex or Jellyfin but Kodi works fine for me now, it's just something I'll need to look at when I move and make my life my own I suppose.

Everything I love has to circle back to the Internet though, and recently learning to appreciate it as a network and system to share and display files instead of a frontend for social media has really helped change my perspective on a lot of things.
